The Film.

Documentary · Theatre of the Oppressed

Boots on Backwards is a feature documentary exploring the false positives scandal in Colombia (2002–2008), where over 6,000 civilians were murdered by military forces and falsely presented as guerrilla fighters killed in combat.

Working with the Mothers of Soacha — bereaved mothers who organised resistance and achieved crimes against humanity recognition — the film connects Colombian state violence to global patterns of military impunity operating in Gaza, Palestine, and Ireland.

The film explores how performance and testimony can maintain present-tense urgency, refusing to sanitise historical violence as safely past.

Currently in development. Production planned 2027.
